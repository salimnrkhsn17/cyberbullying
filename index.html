Stop cyberbullying

About Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying is the use of electronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending messages of an intimidating or threatening nature.

It can happen to anyone, anytime, and anywhere.

Impact of Cyberbullying

Cyberbullying can have serious consequences including:

  • Emotional distress
  • Depression and anxiety
  • Suicidal thoughts
  • Social isolation

Preventing Cyberbullying

Steps to prevent cyberbullying:

  1. Be mindful of what you post online.
  2. Think before you type or share.
  3. Report cyberbullying to the appropriate authorities.
  4. Support victims of cyberbullying.
